    Biography

    Ziwei Zhang received his Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, in 2021. He is currently a postdoc researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. His research interests focus on machine learning on graphs, including graph neural network (GNN), network embedding (a.k.a. network representation learning), and automated graph learning. He has published over 30 papers in prestigious conferences and journals, including ICML, NeurIPS, KDD, AAAI, IJCAI, and TKDE.
